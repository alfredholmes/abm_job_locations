Companies House Locations:
	Companies House API (used in the python script that pulls company movement data):
		https://developer.companieshouse.gov.uk/api/docs/

	Post Codes (postcodes_la.csv - csv that maps postcodes to the local authority code):
		Download:
			https://ons.maps.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=dfa0ff74981b4b228d2030d852f0b14a
			not sure why this is not an ons / gov site

		Link to download from ons / gov site:
			http://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/datasets?q=ONSPD+February+2017&sort_by=name&sort_order=asc
	Company snapshots to get list of company ids (CompanyNumbers2012-2018.csv):
		Most Recent Snapshot:
			http://download.companieshouse.gov.uk/

		Archived snapshots to find dissolved companies - data from 2012 - 2017, 2015 missing
			https://web.archive.org/web/*/http://download.companieshouse.gov.uk/*
			- Enter the search term 'Basic' to find list of the archived snapshots
